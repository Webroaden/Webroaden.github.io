Partners

A list of our highly involved partners!

Medical Directive

The Medical Directive (MD) organization is a large multi-disciplinary network that spans across many institutions. This encompasses high school, undergraduate, graduate and professional, post-graduate, and industry level partners in both public and private sectors. Medical Directive serves as a medium for collaboration among these various stakeholders, and drives innovation in pre-health education and certain applications within the greater medical field. In doing so, MD is the main platform for national members, who have a more significant commitment to the values of the organization as a whole.

Mera Mast Mann

Mera Mast Mann is a mental health app for the South Asian American Youth. The app aims to destigmatize mental health in the community as well as offer theraputics that target the struggles of South Asian-American Youth. The app offers multiple features such as a homepage with notifications, online anonymous forum, meditation resources, and self-therapy guides.
